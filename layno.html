Lightweight Apex-based Enhanced Network (LAENet)

Micro-expression recognition network trained with MatLab.

We developed and trained a MatLab network majorly based on STSTNet which can analyzed a person's facial micro-expression in order to determine category of their emotion (positive, negative, surprised)!

Training- We used Macro-expression dataset (CK+) to train our network(LAENet). Staring with croping faces from each data to make the faces aligned to the center. Then the data underwent processing such as optical flow computation and concatemation etc. After that, imported the processed data to train the network.

Testing- Firstly, we spotted the apex of each data from Micro-expression dataset (SMIC, CASMEII, SAMM) in order to get the frame with most emotion in it. Secondly, evaluated image process like training data but added eye masking steps in order to decrease the noise interferences such as reflection of eye-glasses and blinking. Finally, imported these data to the network and obtained the overall accuracy of 79.19%

Mag Racers

Purdue EPICS Team IS project.

Purdue EPICS is a service-learning design program and team IS works on designing and building interactive museum exhibits based on STEM knowledge for Imagination Station where children near our community can explore science.

Mag Racers is one of the project for Team IS. This exhibit allows children learn about magnetism through the car racing game with their peers. There's two tracks in the exhibit and the tracks are wraping by multiple coils. With the electricity running through the coils generating the magnetic fields which will make the conductive cars in the tracks moving back and forth with attraction and repellation of the magnetic fields.

I was the design lead of the team Jan 2021~ May 2022. Worked on winding coils, wiring, reconstructing the main funciton with the customized PCBs and building/testing reset mechanism using Arduino UNO board. Also held team meeting routinely in order to distribute the works and get updates of each part of the project.
